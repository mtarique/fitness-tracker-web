Understanding TDEE

What is TDEE?

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is the total number of calories you burn in a day, including your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) plus all physical activities. TDEE is crucial for understanding how many calories you need to maintain, lose, or gain weight.

How is TDEE Calculated?

TDEE = BMR × Activity Multiplier

Activity multipliers:

  • 1.2 - Sedentary (little or no exercise)
  • 1.375 - Lightly active (light exercise 1-3 days/week)
  • 1.55 - Moderately active (moderate exercise 3-5 days/week)
  • 1.725 - Very active (hard exercise 6-7 days/week)
  • 1.9 - Extra active (very hard exercise & physical job)
Using TDEE for Goals
  • Maintain weight: Eat calories equal to your TDEE
  • Lose weight: Eat 300-500 calories less than your TDEE
  • Gain weight: Eat 300-500 calories more than your TDEE
