Understanding Ideal Weight

What is Ideal Weight?

Ideal weight is a healthy weight range that considers your height, gender, and body frame. It's based on the Devine formula and adjusted for body frame size. This range helps you set realistic weight goals.

Body Frame Sizes

Determine your frame size:

  • Small: Wrist circumference less than 6.25 inches (men) or 5.75 inches (women)
  • Medium: Wrist circumference 6.25-6.75 inches (men) or 5.75-6.25 inches (women)
  • Large: Wrist circumference greater than 6.75 inches (men) or 6.25 inches (women)

Alternatively, use general build as a guide: small-boned = small, average-boned = medium, large-boned = large

Important Note

Ideal weight is a general guideline. Factors like muscle mass, bone density, and overall body composition also matter. Consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
