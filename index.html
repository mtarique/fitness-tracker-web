About Our Fitness & Health Tracker

Welcome to FitnessTracker — your smart health companion designed to help you understand your body, set realistic goals, and build a healthier lifestyle. Whether you want to track your BMI, manage your weight, or simply stay motivated, FitTrack makes it simple, personal, and data-driven.

By entering just a few details like age, weight, and height, you instantly get your Body Mass Index (BMI) and a personalized health summary. Based on your category — underweight, normal, overweight, or obese — our intelligent system provides practical fitness and nutrition tips tailored to your needs.

We believe that small, consistent steps make the biggest difference. That’s why FitTrack helps you monitor your progress, get AI-powered recommendations, and make healthier choices every day. No complicated setups, no expensive plans — just simple insights for real results.
