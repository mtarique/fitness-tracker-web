Understanding Body Fat Percentage

What is Body Fat Percentage?

Body fat percentage is the proportion of fat to total body weight. It's a better indicator of fitness than BMI alone, as it distinguishes between fat mass and lean body mass.

How to Measure

For accurate results, use a flexible measuring tape:

  • Neck: Measure just below the larynx (Adam's apple)
  • Waist: Measure at the navel level for men, or narrowest point for women
  • Hip (women only): Measure at the widest point of the hips
Body Fat Categories
Men:
  • Essential: 2-5%
  • Athletes: 6-13%
  • Fitness: 14-17%
  • Average: 18-24%
  • Obese: 25%+
Women:
  • Essential: 10-13%
  • Athletes: 14-20%
  • Fitness: 21-24%
  • Average: 25-31%
  • Obese: 32%+
