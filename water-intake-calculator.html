Importance of Proper Hydration

Why Water Matters

Water is essential for numerous bodily functions including temperature regulation, nutrient transport, joint lubrication, and waste removal. Proper hydration improves physical performance, cognitive function, and overall health.

How It's Calculated

Base calculation: Weight (kg) × 35ml = Base water intake

Additional factors:

  • Activity level: Adds extra water for exercise and physical work
  • Climate: Hot and humid conditions increase water needs
  • Pregnancy/Breastfeeding: May require additional water (consult doctor)
Hydration Tips
  • Drink water consistently throughout the day, not just when thirsty
  • Increase intake before, during, and after exercise
  • Monitor urine color - pale yellow indicates good hydration
  • Remember that some foods (fruits, vegetables) also contribute to hydration
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol, as they can increase fluid loss
Note: Individual needs may vary. This is a general guideline. Consult a healthcare professional if you have specific health conditions or concerns.
